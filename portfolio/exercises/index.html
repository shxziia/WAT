INGREDIENTS

For the pastry:

  • 200g plain flour
  • 100g cold butter, diced
  • 50g icing sugar
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 2-3 tbsp cold water

For the filling:

  • 4 eg
  • 4 egg yolks
  • juice of 4 or 5 lemons (about 150ml)
  • 190g caster sugar
  • 60g unsalted butter
  • optional gooseberry jam for glaze

METHOD

For the pastry:

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Combine flour, butter, and icing sugar in a bowl. Rub together until mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
  3. Add egg yolk and cold water, mix until dough forms. Chill for 30 minutes.
  4. Roll out dough and line tart tins. Prick bases and chill again for 10 minutes.
  5. Bake blind for 15 minutes, then remove weights and bake for another 5 minutes until golden.

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (160°C fan).
  2. Roll out the pastry and line tart tins.
  3. Blind bake the pastry for 15 minutes.
  4. Whisk eggs, yolks, sugar, and lemon juice for filling.
  5. Stir in melted butter, pour into pastry cases.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes until just set.
  7. Cool tarts, optionally glaze with gooseberry jam before serving.
