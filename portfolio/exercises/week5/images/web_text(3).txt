Lemon Custard Tarts

Cook
Oven bake / Hob / Chill
Serves
6
Preparation Time
90 minutes
Cooking Time
30 minutes
Level
Medium

lemon custard tart
lemon custard tart

Ingredients
For the pastry:
90g butter
45g caster sugar
grated zest of 1 lemon
1 egg yolk
120g plain flour

For the filling:
4 eggs
4 egg yolks
juice of 4 or 5 lemons (about 150ml)
190g caster sugar
60g unsalted butter
optional gooseberry jam to glaze

Method
Make the pastry:
Mix butter, sugar and lemon zest together
Add egg yolk and mix well
Sift in flour and mix to a dough
Wrap in cling film and chill for 45 minutes
Roll out dough on floured surface and line tart tins
Preheat oven to 180°C and blind bake for 15 minutes
                        
Make the filling:
Whisk eggs, egg yolks, lemon juice and sugar together in a bowl until well combined
Pour mixture into a saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring constantly until thickened (do not boil) - about 15 minutes
Remove from heat and stir in butter until melted and smooth
Pour filling into the baked tart shells
Once cooled, chill in the fridge for at least 2 hours
Optional: brush tops with warmed gooseberry jam for a glossy finish
For a caramelized top, sprinkle with sugar and use a kitchen blowtorch to melt and brown the sugar
